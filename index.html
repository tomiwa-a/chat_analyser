How it works

Three Simple Steps

01

Export Your Chat

Open WhatsApp, select a chat, tap "More" → "Export chat" → "Without Media". Save the .zip or .txt file.

02

Drop & Analyze

Drag your file into ChatLens. Our advanced parser handles all date formats and extracts every message.

03

Explore Insights

View beautiful charts, stats, and patterns. Discover who sends the most emojis and when you're most active.
